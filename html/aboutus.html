ABOUT US

Our Story

We at ‘Nidhi Wellness’ strongly believe that ‘true healing comes by nourishing of the mind, the body and the soul.’ We are a team of highly motivated fitness experts and are committed towards spreading the positive energy of a healthy mind and a pure soul, residing in a fit body. All-round regular practice and training is the only way to a truly happy life.

Nidhi Wellness has been founded by Nidhi Kumar who is a qualified Dietitian, Nutritionist and Yoga Alliance certified Yoga instructor. Nidhi Kumar has been actively involved in the field of health and fitness since the year 2005. She has more than 15 years of experience in the fitness industry and thousands of satisfied customers. She is a military spouse, married to a pilot serving in the ‘Indian Air Force’ and is on a mission to spread the message of all-round fitness all over the world.
